Overview

This small site provides a quick reference for common livestock types, their basic needs, and friendly resources for new and experienced keepers.

Animals

Cattle

Grazers that require pasture, clean water, and regular health checks. Ideal for milk, meat, or draft depending on breed.

Sheep

Smaller grazers good for wool and meat. Require secure fencing and parasite management.

Pigs

Omnivorous animals that need rooting space and a sturdy shelter. Excellent for meat production and waste management.
